About Me

Hi i'm Bella!

I'm a full stack developer, Maths graduate and newbie running enthusiast.

I first learnt to code when in my third year at university. I quickly saw the logical approach needed, and realised that my problem solving skills, developed after years of solving complicated mathematical problems, would help me enormously when trying to come up with logical solutions to tasks I undertake and fix any bugs that may show up in my code (which nine times out of ten turned out to be a silly typo, thankfully I discovered code spell checker extensions).

Once I finished University I knew I wanted to follow a career in software development, and that I wanted to learn as much as I could, as fast as I could. After weighing up the pros and cons of the possible paths available to me, I settled on a bootcamp, so that I would also learn work place best practices such as version control and Test Driven Development (TDD), as well as practice skills I touched on while learning Python at University, such as pair-programming and remote collaboration.

Having completed Northcoders' software development bootcamp (nearly). I am looking to take the next step and put my new skills to the test by starting a Junior Software developer role.
